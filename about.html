Hello, I'm Syamil 👋

I'm a third-year Computer Science student at Universiti Sultan Zainal Abidin (UniSZA), specialising in software development. I created Inkwell as a space to document what I learn, share honest reflections on the tech industry, and practice communicating complex ideas clearly.

Writing helps me think. When I have to explain a concept to an imaginary reader, I quickly find the gaps in my own understanding. This blog is both a portfolio and a learning journal — imperfect by design, genuine by intention.

What I Do

By day I'm learning about algorithms, databases, and software architecture. By night I build small web projects, experiment with CSS, and occasionally contribute to open source. My current focus is on:

  • → Frontend development with semantic HTML, modern CSS, and vanilla JavaScript
  • → Version control best practices and open source collaboration via GitHub
  • → Writing and documenting technical work clearly for non-technical audiences
  • → Understanding UX principles and applying them to student projects

Why "Inkwell"?

An inkwell is where ideas are dipped before they become words on a page. It felt like the right metaphor for a place where half-formed thoughts get sharpened into something coherent. Also, the name was available. 🙂
